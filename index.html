Step 2: Understanding Organic Ranking

When someone in the DFW area searches online for a hair salon or a specific service like balayage, search engines such as Google or Yahoo decide which websites show up first. This order, called organic ranking, is determined by multiple factors:

Content

Technical SEO

Backlinks

Engagement

Ranking

By improving in these areas, Carolina & Company Salon can build credibility with search engines and steadily rise in organic rankings for the services it wants to highlight.

Step 4: Analysis of Current Web Pages

Strengths

  • Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across pages
  • Wide variety of services listed
  • Team page highlights stylist specialties
  • Integrated booking system through Square/Vagaro

Weaknesses

  • All services grouped on one page; no dedicated service pages
  • Generic titles and meta descriptions
  • Typos in service copy; missing structured headings; no schema markup
  • Weak local SEO (no map embed, few neighborhood mentions)

Recommendations

  • Create dedicated service pages (Balayage, Blowouts, Extensions, Brows/Lashes)
  • Rewrite titles/meta descriptions with Dallas + service keywords
  • Add local SEO elements (Google Map, Oak Cliff/Bishop Arts mentions)
  • Add schema markup (LocalBusiness/HairSalon)
  • Fix typos and optimize content with SEO-friendly H1/H2
  • Strengthen internal linking (homepage → services, stylists → relevant services)

Step 5: Inbound Links & Other SEO Recommendations

To compete with top Dallas salons, Carolina & Company Salon needs to build a stronger backlink profile. The goal is to secure 30–50 new inbound links from 10–15 unique domains within the next year.

Link Building Roadmap

Local Directories Blogs Press Partnerships Reviews

Specific Recommendations:

  • Add listings on Google Business, Yelp, Bing Places, Nextdoor, Dallas Chamber of Commerce
  • Partner with Dallas beauty/lifestyle bloggers
  • Pitch to Dallas Observer, D Magazine, lifestyle press
  • Collaborate with local influencers on Instagram/YouTube
  • Encourage client reviews on Yelp, Google, and Facebook
